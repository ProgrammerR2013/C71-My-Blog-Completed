Places Must Visit

suruchi restaurant

i had visited in 19th june 2022

This is a pleasant restaurant which is based in Sangli, Maharasthra. It has very good food quality, spice level and many other food properties. It is very hygenic and clean and has fast service.



elephanta caves

i had visited in 5th August 2022

Elephanta Caves is an UNESCO world heritage site and is a very good tourist spot. This is just a 1km boat ride from the Gateway of India in Mumbai, and the best part is you get to sit in a very cool train while travelling to the caves! The boat ride just cost 50 rupees, please visit it!


Movies You Should Watch

Avengers Endgame

Best movie ever made by MCU!

"Avengers Endgame is a excellent movies and is the best movie made by MCU Universe Productions. This revolves around the avengers fighting a villain to get 6 magical stones which can bring back people from the dead so they can revive dead avengers. To have fun in this movie you need to watch the other movied in the series for example Black Panther, Avengers: Infinity war and Avengers: Age of Ultron. I suggest to watch this movie very soon because this is a very fun action-packed adventure movie!


Lion King

Good animated movies for kids and good story!

"Lion King" is a very good animated movie for kids and has a good plot. The story revolves around a growing lion named Simba who is crown prince of the jungle but things go wrong when Simba becomes enemies with the fierce hyenas. This is a adventurous movie and it is very fun to watch the fights and the songs. I suggest you to watch the movie before it gets out-dated!
